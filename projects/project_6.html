Concept Models

Below are some ideas and concepts of popular consumer goods I have made.

Here is my rendering of what I think the iPhone 12 might look like. I really liked the form factor of the iPhone 5, and tried to reintroduce the boxy-aluminum frame. Keeping up with the trends seen in smartphones recently, I also added a smaller notch, and a 4-camera setup in the back.

This is my idea of what a really cool set of BMW rims would look like. Keeping up with the companies design code, I have mimicked the overall shape of the wheel while adding some modern touches. The major differences include modifying the shape of the spokes to stand out a bit more, having slightly more negative space, and the cherry on top: painting everything matte black!

Most computer users can agree that having more monitors is better, so I was inspired to design this monitor arm. This arm functions by being mountable to a table and accepts most common monitor mounting patterns. The arm has 360 degrees of rotation due to slip rings at the joints, and can "fold" on itself to provide a small footprint.
